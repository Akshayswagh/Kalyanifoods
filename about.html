About Kalyani Ice Cream Parlour

"Swaad Jo Sadaa Rahe Yaad."

Welcome to Our Parlour

Welcome to Kalyani Ice Cream Parlour, a paradise for ice cream lovers since 1994. We are known for crafting premium, delicious, and innovative ice creams that delight every customer. From classic flavors to unique blends, our parlour is where your sweet dreams come true.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound desire: to create smiles through the irresistible joy of ice cream. Every scoop reflects our commitment to quality, tradition, and a sprinkle of innovation.

Our Journey

Established in the heart of our community, Kalyani Ice Cream Parlour started as a small shop with a big dream: to create smiles through the joy of ice cream. Over the years, we have grown, but our commitment to quality and passion for ice cream remain the same.

We've watched families grow, celebrated countless milestones, and become a beloved part of local traditions, all while serving up unforgettable sweet treats since 1994.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To spread happiness with every scoop. By using the finest ingredients and staying true to our artisanal roots, we aim to be your go-to destination for the best ice creams in town.

Our Vision

To innovate and offer a wide range of flavors, from timeless classics to seasonal delights, ensuring there's something for everyone. We constantly explore new combinations.

Why Choose Us?

Wide Range of Flavours

Discover an extensive selection of classic and unique ice cream flavors to satisfy every craving.

Made Fresh Daily

Enjoy peak freshness and flavor with our ice creams, churned and prepared fresh every day.

No Artificial Additives

Relish pure taste. Our recipes are free from artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives.

High-Quality Dairy

We use only the finest milk and cream to ensure a rich, creamy texture and superior taste.

A Part of the Community

At Kalyani Ice Cream Parlour, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us for decades. From sponsoring local events to hosting ice cream parties for schools and organizations, we are proud to contribute to the happiness of our neighbors.

We're more than just an ice cream shop; we're a gathering place, a local landmark, and a source of sweet memories for many.
